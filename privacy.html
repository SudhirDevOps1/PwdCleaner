Privacy Policy

🔒 100% Private

Last updated: January 2024

No Data Collection

Password Manager Duplicate Cleaner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Period.

No Tracking

This application contains:

  • Zero analytics scripts
  • Zero tracking cookies
  • Zero external requests
  • Zero third-party scripts
  • Zero fingerprinting

Client-Side Processing

All password import, parsing, duplicate detection, and export operations happen entirely in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

Local Storage

If you enable the encryption feature, your password data is encrypted using AES-GCM with PBKDF2 key derivation (100,000 iterations) and stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and can only be decrypted with your master passphrase.

Service Worker

The service worker caches static assets for offline use only. It does not collect or transmit any data.

Open Source

This application is fully open source. You can review every line of code to verify these privacy claims.

Contact

If you have privacy concerns, contact us at developer@example.com
